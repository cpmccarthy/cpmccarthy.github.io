TUSCAN LEMON CHICKEN

  • 1â€“2 large chicken breast
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp grated lemon zest
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 3 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tps dried rosemary
  • salt and pepper as desired

Marinade

Combine all the ingredients together in a bowl and mix until all the ingredients are evenly distributed. Then pour over the uncooked chicken, and rub the marinade in. Marinade overnight (highly recommended) or for at least 8 hours, turning once.

Chicken

Preheat your gill to 350. Once preheated place the chicken on the grill and place a meat thermometer in the chicken. Leave the chicken on the grill until the juices run clear and the internal temperature reaches 180 degrees.

Serve the chicken warm
